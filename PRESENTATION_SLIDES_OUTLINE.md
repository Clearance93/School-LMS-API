## PRESENTATION TIPS

### Opening (First 2 Minutes)
- Start with a compelling story or statistic
- Establish credibility quickly
- Set clear expectations for the presentation
- Create emotional connection to the problem

### Middle (Core Content)
- Use the "Problem-Solution-Proof" framework
- Show, don't just tell (use demos if possible)
- Keep slides visual, not text-heavy
- Tell stories, not just facts
- Pause for questions at key points

### Closing (Last 5 Minutes)
- Summarize key benefits
- Restate the ROI clearly
- Create urgency (limited offer, market timing)
- Make the call to action crystal clear
- End with confidence and enthusiasm

### Body Language & Delivery
- Maintain eye contact
- Use open gestures
- Move with purpose
- Vary your tone and pace
- Show passion and enthusiasm
- Smile and be personable

### Handling Objections
- Listen fully before responding
- Acknowledge the concern
- Provide specific evidence
- Turn objection into opportunity
- Never be defensive

### Time Management
- 30-minute version: Slides 1-2, 4-7, 11-13, 19, 22, 25
- 45-minute version: All slides except 20-21
- 60-minute version: All slides with demo
- Always leave 10 minutes for Q&A
